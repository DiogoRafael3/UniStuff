Original Reprodução

Sergeant Major Kururu is the head of communications in the Keroro Platoon. His body is yellow, with orange headgear that includes two headphone-like white pieces over his ears. His forehead and belly mark is a filled spiral. He wears glasses with spirals on them. Kururu was blue when he was little, but at the end of the short movie Secret of the Kero Ball, he became yellow, due to being stuck in a huge plate of curry that Keroro made for him from the Kero Ball.
